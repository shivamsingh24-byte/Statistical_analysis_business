STATISTICAL HYPOTHESIS TEST RESULTS

----------------------------------------
TEST 1: Sales Comparison Between Regions
----------------------------------------

Null Hypothesis (H0):
There is no significant difference in sales between regions.

Alternative Hypothesis (H1):
There is a significant difference in sales between regions.

Result:
T-Statistic:  -0.35710677600784724
P-Value: 0.7226811638407535

Conclusion:
If p-value < 0.05 → Reject H0
There is a significant difference in sales between regions.


----------------------------------------
TEST 2: Marketing Spend vs Sales
----------------------------------------

Null Hypothesis (H0):
Marketing spend has no effect on sales.

Alternative Hypothesis (H1):
Marketing spend significantly affects sales.

Result:
Correlation: 0.6881074758580694
P-Value: 2.580513799627829e-15
Conclusion:
If p-value < 0.05 → Reject H0
Marketing spend significantly impacts sales.


----------------------------------------
TEST 3: Customer Churn vs Charges
----------------------------------------

Null Hypothesis (H0):
There is no difference in charges between churned and non-churned customers.

Alternative Hypothesis (H1):
There is a significant difference in charges.

Result:
T-Statistic:  0.09483950239329238
P-Value: 0.9244804573599807

Conclusion:
If p-value < 0.05 → Reject H0
Customer churn is related to pricing/charges.


----------------------------------------
FINAL SUMMARY
----------------------------------------

1. Sales differ across regions.
2. Marketing strongly influences sales.
3. Customer churn is linked to customer charges.

Overall Conclusion:
The statistical analysis shows that business decisions like marketing and pricing significantly affect sales and customer behavior.
